# How did the Battle of the Somme progress

Evidence of British Success:
- Had 6x the amount of German troops
- Germans had no counter-attack prepared
- 37,200 wounded in the first day before noon
- 19,240 killed in the first day before noon
- 80,000 Germans captures
- Creeping Barage - Fire cannons and march forward behind
- Tanks
- Air Observation

Evidence of British Failure:
- 1st day: 20,000 killed
- German Machine guns
- First Push Failed
- Second Push Failed
- Commanders were 3 miles away from the battlefield
- Commanders kept their plans static
- Third Push Failed
